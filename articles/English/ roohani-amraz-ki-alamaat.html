General Signs of Spiritual Unease

Certain signs are traditionally attributed to "spiritual disturbance" in traditional spiritual sciences. However, it is important to understand that most of these signs can also have physical or psychological causes.

Commonly Cited Signs

Unexplained unease, disturbed sleep, recurring nightmares, and unusual lethargy are traditionally sometimes attributed to spiritual disturbance.

Recommended Treatment

In such situations, reciting the Qur'an — particularly Al-Mu'awwidhatayn and Ayat al-Kursi — and frequent seeking of forgiveness is recommended. All these practices bring peace and contentment to the heart.

A Necessary Precaution

If symptoms persist or intensify, do not rely on spiritual treatment alone — also consult a qualified physician or mental health professional. Spiritual and medical treatment can proceed together.
