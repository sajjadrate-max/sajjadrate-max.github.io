The Signs of Envy and Its Spiritual Remedy

Envy (hasad) is a feeling in which a person, upon seeing another's blessing, desires its decline. Islam regards envy as a deeply blameworthy trait.

Signs of Envy

Feeling uneasy at others' happiness and success, speaking ill of them, and inwardly wishing for their loss are common signs of envy.

Recommended Supplications for Protection from Envy

Surah Al-Falaq specifically seeks refuge from the harm of an envier. Ayat al-Kursi and the recommended morning and evening remembrances are also considered a means of protection from envy and the evil eye.

Guarding Against Envy

One is taught to keep the heart free of envy by feeling happy for others' blessings and praying for them. This practice brings not only spiritual peace but also strengthens social relationships.
