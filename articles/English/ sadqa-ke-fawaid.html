The Spiritual and Social Benefits of Charity

Charity (sadqah) is among the most beloved acts of worship in Islam. Countless virtues and benefits of giving charity are described in the Qur'an and Hadith.

Spiritual Benefits

It is traditionally believed that charity wards off calamities and serves as protection from misfortune. The Hadith also describe charity as an expiation for sins and a cause of blessing in provision.

Social Benefits

Charity carries spiritual benefit for the giver and also helps reduce poverty in society and strengthens mutual compassion. It helps narrow the gap between rich and poor.

Types of Charity

Charity is not limited to giving wealth alone — speaking a good word, greeting with a smile, and removing a harmful object from the road are all also forms of charity. Every good deed counts as charity in some form.
