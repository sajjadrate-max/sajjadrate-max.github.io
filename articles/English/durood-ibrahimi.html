The Virtues of Durood Ibrahimi

Durood Ibrahimi is the specific blessing recited during the sitting (tashahhud) of prayer, in which mercy is sought upon the Prophet (peace be upon him) and his family in the same manner as it was sent upon Prophet Ibrahim (peace be upon him) and his family.

Virtue

The Hadith describe countless virtues of reciting Durood. It is said that whoever recites Durood abundantly receives Allah's mercy in abundance and has their sins forgiven.

Making It Part of Daily Routine

Many scholars recommend reciting Durood at least a few times after every prayer. This practice not only brings spiritual peace but also expresses love for the Prophet (peace be upon him).

Summary

Durood should be a regular part of every Muslim's daily life. Rather than limiting it to a specific time or count, reciting it as often as possible is considered the recommended practice.
