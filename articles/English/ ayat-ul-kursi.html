The Virtues and Effect of Ayat al-Kursi

Ayat al-Kursi is the 255th verse of Surah Al-Baqarah and is regarded as the greatest verse in the Qur'an. It describes Allah's oneness, greatness, and boundless power.

Traditional Virtues

The Hadith describe Ayat al-Kursi as a means of protection. It is traditionally recommended to recite it morning and evening, before sleeping, and when leaving the house, in order to attain Allah's protection.

The Belief in Spiritual Protection

Many people recite it for protection from the evil eye, whispers, and spiritual unease. This is a centuries-old recommended practice widely observed among Muslims.

An Important Point

Ayat al-Kursi should be recited with faith and conviction. Alongside this, taking appropriate precautions and seeking suitable medical or psychological help in daily life is equally important.
