Principles of Healing Through the Holy Qur'an

Allah Himself has described the Qur'an as "shifa" — a healing. Surah Al-Isra states that We send down through the Qur'an that which is a healing and a mercy for the believers.

The Meaning of Spiritual Healing

The Qur'an is considered a source of spiritual healing for the ailments of the heart — such as envy, anger, unease, and heedlessness. Its recitation, reflection, and practice purify a person's inner self.

Traditional Method

Traditionally, certain specific chapters and verses (such as Surah Al-Fatiha, Al-Mu'awwidhatayn, and Ayat al-Kursi) are recited specifically for healing, whether blown over water that is then drunk or recited directly.

Not a Substitute for Medical Treatment

It is important to understand that attaining spiritual healing from the Qur'an is not a substitute for medical treatment. In the case of any physical illness, seeking treatment from a qualified physician is fully in keeping with Islamic teaching as well.
