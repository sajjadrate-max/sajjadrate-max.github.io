An Islamic Introduction to Dream Interpretation

The Hadith divide dreams into three categories: true dreams that come from Allah, satanic dreams that create distress and fear, and psychological dreams that reflect a person's everyday thoughts.

In the Case of a Good Dream

If a good dream occurs, it is recommended to thank Allah for it and share it only with good and trustworthy people.

In the Case of a Bad Dream

If a frightening or distressing dream occurs, one is advised to seek refuge from Satan and is advised not to narrate it to anyone.

Caution in Interpretation

Dream interpretation is a delicate science in which error is possible. Therefore, interpretation should only be sought from qualified, knowledgeable individuals, and caution should be exercised before making important decisions based on a dream.
